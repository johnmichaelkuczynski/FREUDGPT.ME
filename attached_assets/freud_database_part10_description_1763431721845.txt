THEORETICAL EVOLUTION VISIBLE IN PART 10:
=========================================

Part 10 represents important post-WWI developments (1918-1919):

1. CLINICAL CONSOLIDATION: Wolf Man case demonstrates clinical application of all major theoretical developments through 1917, showing complex interplay of primal scene, deferred action, regression, and multiple neurotic organizations.

2. TECHNIQUE REFINEMENT: Lines of Advance articulates mature therapeutic principles including abstinence, active intervention for specific disorders, and vision for public mental health applications.

3. PERVERSION THEORY: "A Child is Being Beaten" provides systematic account of perversion development through three-phase phantasy evolution, connecting early fixation, guilt, regression, and symptom formation.

4. APPLIED EXPANSION: The Uncanny extends psychoanalytic insights to aesthetics, showing how return of repressed operates in artistic and literary experience.

5. THEORETICAL QUESTIONS: Wolf Man case explicitly addresses unresolved question of primal phantasies versus actual observation, leaving it as "non liquet" (unresolved).
